How Save-A-Bite Works

Four simple steps from surplus food to served meals.

1

Donor Lists Food

Restaurants, individuals & companies post surplus food with expiry details and pickup location.

2

Smart Matching

Our algorithm matches donations to the nearest NGO based on urgency, quantity & reliability.

3

Volunteer Picks Up

A volunteer accepts the delivery task and picks up from the donor location.

4

Meals Served

Food reaches the NGO and is distributed to people in need. Impact is recorded.
